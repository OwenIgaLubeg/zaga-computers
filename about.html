About ZAGA Technologies

Innovating Digital Solutions for Africa’s Growing Tech Landscape

Who We Are

ZAGA Technologies is a forward-thinking IT company based in Uganda, specializing in providing cutting-edge hardware and software solutions to empower individuals, startups, and enterprises. From laptops and smartphones to network systems and office printers, we strive to make technology accessible and reliable for everyone. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by bringing innovative and affordable tech closer to businesses and homes across Africa.

Financing Digital Empowerment — Buy Now & Pay Later

At ZAGA Technologies, we believe financial limitations shouldn’t stop anyone from owning quality technology. Our “Buy Now & Pay Later” program allows customers to access gadgets and pay in flexible installments — making digital empowerment achievable for all. We partner with financial institutions to ensure every purchase is secure, transparent, and designed around our customers’ convenience.
